SOFA is a small but passionate furniture company founded with a single belief — that good furniture transforms a house into a home. We source our materials from sustainable forests and partner with skilled craftspeople to bring you pieces that are as beautiful as they are durable.

Every item in our collection is handpicked by our design team. From the clean lines of our Scandinavian-inspired sofas to the rich warmth of our teak dining tables, we obsess over every detail so you don't have to.

We deliver pan-India, offer free shipping on orders above ₹5,000, and back every product with a 2-year warranty. Our 24/7 support team is always just a message away.
